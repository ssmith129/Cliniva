help_outline How It Works?

Cliniva provides a robust, built-in multi-role management system. This architecture allows you to maintain a single codebase while serving tailored experiences for different user types. By default, it includes three core roles: Admin, Doctor, and Patient.

Admin Role

Full system authority and comprehensive management capabilities.

The Admin is the primary role responsible for managing and controlling all aspects of the hospital. This includes medical staff management, patient oversight, financial operations, and system-wide configurations.

Doctor Role

Focused on medical excellence and patient care.

The Doctor role is designed for medical staff to manage their daily schedules, track patient progress, and deliver medical care efficiently. It provides tools for appointment management and assessment.

Patient Role

Personalized portal for health and treatment management.

The Patient role offers a comprehensive view of the health journey. Patients can access their medical records, track treatment progress, manage bill payments, and stay updated with hospital announcements.
