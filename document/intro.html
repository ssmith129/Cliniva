info Introductions

Cliniva is a comprehensive hospital management admin template built with Angular 21+ and Angular Material Design. Designed specifically for hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions, Cliniva provides a complete solution for managing all aspects of healthcare operations with 300+ ready-to-use pages, 200+ reusable UI components, and 13 AI-powered features.

Built with modern web technologies including SASS preprocessor, HTML5, and CSS3, Cliniva delivers a fully responsive experience that works seamlessly across all devices - from desktop workstations to tablets and mobile devices. This ensures healthcare professionals can access critical information and manage operations from anywhere, at any time.

The template follows a multi-role architecture with three distinct portals: Admin (34 feature modules), Doctor (18 feature modules), and Patient (20 feature modules), each with tailored dashboards and functionality. Built with standalone components, zoneless change detection, and lazy-loaded routes for optimal performance.

  • check_circleRole-Based Multi-Portal: Dedicated dashboards and modules for Admin, Doctors, and Patients with granular permission control.

  • check_circleAppointment Management: Full calendar-based scheduling, online booking, doctor availability, patient queues, and automated reminders.
  • check_circleElectronic Medical Records (EMR): Complete patient encounters, clinical notes, treatment plans, medication history, and document management.
  • check_circlePharmacy Management: Full inventory with POS system, prescription queue, drug interaction checker, stock alerts, supplier orders, and expiry tracking.
  • check_circleLaboratory & Radiology: Test catalog management, sample collection tracking, technician assignment, result entry, imaging reports, and scan tracking.
  • check_circleBilling & Accounts: Invoice generation, payment receipts, expense tracking, revenue dashboard, tax reports, and comprehensive insurance claim management.
  • check_circleTelemedicine: Video consultations, virtual visit records, waiting room management, consultation scheduling, and post-consultation notes with analytics.
  • check_circleOperation Theatre (OT): Surgery scheduling, pre-op assessments, post-op notes, surgical records, and OT-specific inventory management.
  • check_circleICU Management: Bed management, patient vitals monitoring, ventilator logs, nursing notes, and ICU-specific dashboard with real-time status.
  • check_circleHuman Resources: Employee attendance, leave management, payroll, payslip generation, ID card generator, shift management, and performance tracking.
  • check_circlePWA Ready: Progressive Web App support with service worker caching, offline capability, and installable app manifest.

auto_awesomeAI Features Overview

Cliniva integrates 13 AI-powered features leveraging OpenAI GPT and Google Gemini models to enhance clinical decision-making and operational efficiency:

  • smart_toy Global: AI Chat Assistant, AI Smart Insights
  • smart_toy Admin: AI Triage Advisory, AI ICU Forecaster, AI Diet Plan Generator, AI Clinical Notes Scribe, AI Discharge Summary Writer
  • smart_toy Doctor: AI Patient Health Summary, AI Prescription Scribe & Drug Safety
  • smart_toy Patient: AI Vitals Anomaly Detector, AI Lab Report Interpreter, AI Doctor-Speak Explainer, AI Diet & Habits Planner
